Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a fast?

Tap the "Start Fasting" button on the main screen. Select your desired fasting duration (16 hours is default) and start tracking!

Can I change the fasting duration?

Yes! Tap on the duration display (e.g., "16 Hours") to see all available options including 12h, 14h, 16h, 18h, 20h, 23h (OMAD), or create a custom duration.

Will the timer work if I close the app?

Absolutely! Your fast continues even if the app is closed, your phone is locked, or your device restarts. You'll receive a notification when your fast is complete.

Can I end my fast early?

Yes, tap the "End Fast" button if you need to stop before completing your full fasting period. Your actual fasting time will be saved in your history.

How do I view my fasting history?

Tap the "History" tab at the bottom to see all your completed fasts, current streak, average duration, and success rate.

Where is my data stored?

All your data is stored locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any of your information. Your data is completely private and under your control.

Does this app work with Apple Health?

Not in the current version. Apple Health integration is planned for a future update!

Is there an Apple Watch version?

Not yet, but we're considering it for future updates based on user feedback!

The app isn't working correctly. What should I do?

Try these steps:

  1. Close and restart the app
  2. Restart your iPhone
  3. Make sure you have the latest version from the App Store
  4. If problems persist, email us with details
